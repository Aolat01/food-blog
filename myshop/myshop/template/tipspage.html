Tips and Kitchen Hacks

Easy Garlic Peeling

Soak garlic cloves in warm water for 10 minutes. The skin comes off easily without sticky fingers!

Keep Herbs Fresh

Wrap fresh herbs in a damp paper towel and store them in a zip-lock bag in the fridge.

DIY Knife Sharpener

No sharpener? Use the bottom of a ceramic mug to sharpen your kitchen knife in a pinch.
